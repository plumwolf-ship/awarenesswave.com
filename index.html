Facts

🧠 Mental Health

1 in 8 people worldwide live with a mental health disorder like anxiety or depression. Many people have heard about mental health but lack the true means to understand it properly.

💙 Support

Depression can affect a person's mood, energy, sleep, and ability to function daily. It is more than just feeling sad—it can deeply impact work, school, and relationships.

🌱 Healing

Anxiety disorders involve intense, excessive worry or fear that interferes with daily life. Symptoms include restlessness, rapid heartbeat, and difficulty concentrating.

🤝 Awareness

Many individuals hesitate to talk about mental health due to stigma. Open conversations help reduce shame and encourage support.

💪 Recovery

Recognizing warning signs early can make a big difference in recovery. Seeking help from professionals and support systems is key to healing and resilience.
